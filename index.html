WHO ARE WE

The idea for my lip balm bracelet business came to me after a long day of beach fun on holiday, sitting around a campfire with friends when I found myself without my lip balm and wishing for a more convenient and stylish way to carry it with me at all times. When we got back to our tent that night I said to my hubby, I have an amazing idea and spent the rest of the night thinking of the endless possibilities! (I didn't get a wink of sleep that night between my excitement and my 2 month old hehe). Over the next week I researched until my fingers cramped and discovered there was nothing else like it on the market. And just like that Balmy was born!
